The Founders
Meet HER
Donna & Ayesha

Donna Farra and Ayesha Shaheryar are the co‑founders of Noor Collective, a nonprofit Muslim women's wellness club based in Austin, Texas. Donna, a Pediatric Speech‑Language Pathologist Assistant, and Ayesha, a Pediatric Psychiatrist, both witnessed through their work with children and mothers the profound need for holistic support — emotional, physical, and spiritual — for women, especially those carrying the weight of caregiving.

Motivated by their shared passion for fitness and personal growth, they launched Noor Collective to create safe and inspiring spaces where Muslim women can invest in their well‑being — feeling motivated, confident, and proud in their Muslim identity while accessing fitness and wellness opportunities that were previously difficult to find.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a space where movement becomes joy, habits become healing, and holistic wellness becomes a way of life. We're here to support Muslim women in reconnecting with their bodies, building lasting self‑love, forming friendships, and growing together in sisterhood.

Our Vision

A vibrant community where women move with confidence, never feel isolated and alone, and show up in the world rooted in their inner light as their most powerful, authentic selves.
